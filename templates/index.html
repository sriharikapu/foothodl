Text-message based interface

Those in need may have limited access to technology or ability to learn how to use an application. FootHodl only requires its members to have access to receiving text messages.

Powered by network effects

A network of volunteers can use a simple online burner wallet to redeem donated funds. This adds accountability to the system, and provides volunteers with a way to earn UNICEF BOOST tokens for their continued participation.

The benefits of blockchain

Despite its simplicity, Foothodl runs on the blockchain and gets the included benefits of global transparency, and full interoperability with the decentralized finance ecosystem
